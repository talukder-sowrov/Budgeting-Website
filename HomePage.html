Budget Application

    How to use this budgeting application:
  1. Select the Type of Income or Expense for Budget
  2. Enter all information into the box below.
  3. Click Enter and see information on your budgeting and where you can improve.

Income is how much money you earn from something like a job or an investment. Having enough income for your lifestyle is key when attempting to budget your money.

Savings is one of the most important parts of budgeting. Savings allow a person to have enough money in case they face unexpected costs in the future. It is important for people budgeting to have a consistent flow of money towards their savings so that they have money when they are in great need.

Necessities are an important part of living a stable life. Having a set amount of money decided for buying necessities prevents a person from overspending on food and clothing that they didn't initially plan for. While it is important to also have money to buy nicer clothing, having a baseline for necessities is important when trying to budget money.
